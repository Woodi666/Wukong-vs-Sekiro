Digital Technology's Expression of Traditional Martial Arts Culture

Research Background: Why Compare These Two Games?

The comparison between "Black Myth: Wukong" and "Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice" holds unique value. Both are high-quality action RPGs (Souls-like ARPG) but represent different martial arts traditions: "Black Myth: Wukong" is based on Chinese traditional culture and Journey to the West mythology, while "Sekiro" embodies Japanese Bushido spirit and Sengoku period culture. Their similarity in genre (precise combat systems, realistic action representation) combined with their cultural differences provides an ideal sample for studying how digital technology presents traditional martial arts culture. In the current global gaming market, "Sekiro" has proven the commercial potential of traditional cultural-themed games, while "Black Myth" represents a new digital attempt at Chinese martial arts culture.
